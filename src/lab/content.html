Welcome to Strength-of-Materials lab
Introduction

Welcome!

Strength of material is the body of knowledge which deals with the relation between internal forces, deformations and external loads etc. This laboratory provides the basic knowledge of strength of materials and here, the students can perform different tests on different materials.

Click on “List of experiments” to see which are the labs available to explore.



List of experiments
  • Izod Impact Test
  • Charpy Impact Test
  • Direct Shear Test On Mild Steel Rod
  • Direct Shear Test On Wood
  • Direct Shear Test On Mild Steel Plate
  • Brinell Hardness Test
  • Mohr's circle
  • Bending Stresses
  • Deflection in Cantilever
  • Deflection in Beam


Target Audience

This course is primarily for pre-final and/or final year students of Bachelor's course in civil Engineering.

This course is also useful for diploma in civil Engineering students.

Courses Aligned

Following are the courses in which one can come across the theory connected to the experiments in this lab

  • Strength of Materials
  • Mechanics Material

Pre-Requisites

Pre-requisites

This lab is related to a basic course on strength of materials. There are no mandatory pre-requisites however a good understanding of basics of Strength of material subject is required.

Software Requirement

Adobe Flash Plugin is needed to run some of the simulation experiments on the browser. You can download it for free here. Kindly wait for flash file to load when opening a simulation. Depending on your internet connection speed and the number of users accessing our site it may take a minute.

Please use latest Mozilla Firefox or Google Chrome browser for best results.
