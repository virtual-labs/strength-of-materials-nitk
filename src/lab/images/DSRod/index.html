DIRECT SHEAR TEST ON MILD STEEL ROD

Objective

To determine experimentally , the ultimate shear strength in double shear of mild steel rod.

Apparatus used:

Universal testing machine, Shear attachments,Vernier calipers.

STEP  1 

 Take the mild steel rod specimen and determine the diameter of the specimen using vernier calipers.

STEP    2 

 The mild steel rod is placed in the shear attachments and placed into the Universal Testing machine.

STEP    3 

 The machine is switched ON & the load is gradually increased until the specimen is fractured and the required load is noted down.

STEP   4 

 Calculate the double shear strength on mild steel rod.
