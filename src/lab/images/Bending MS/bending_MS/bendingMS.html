BENDING TEST ON MILD STEEL ROD

Objective :

To study the behavior of the mild steel rod subjected to a gradual increasing equal loads at 1/3rd span and to determine mechanical properties.


Apparatus used:

Universal Testing Machine,Dial Indicators,Scale & Vernier calipers

STEP  1 

Measure the Initial Diameter of the test sample in two perpendicular directions using vernier caliper, calculate the initial area and Moment of Inertia.

STEP  2 

Keeping the span of the beam as L = 690mm based on the limitation of the experimental setup and measure the Length of the specimen using scale.

STEP  3 

Based on the span of the beam being L = 690mm mark on the test sample by using a chalk at the mid-span and one-third loading points where a dial gauge is placed then the 2 point loading will be applied at these points.

STEP  4 

Insert the specimen between the 2-Point loading setup and supported on the roller supports which is fixed on the lower crosshead of the Universal Testing Machine. Adjust all the dial gauges to zero.

STEP  5 

Start the loading process, note down the deflections using dial gauges under mid-span, one-third span.Stop the experiment once the specimen has been yielded.
