Fluid Mechanics Lab

Bending test
Saturday, 24 October 2009 18:27

Objective

  • To study the behavior of the mild steel specimen subjected to a gradually increasing equal loads at 1/3rd span and that of wood specimen when subjected to gradually increasing central concentrated load.
  • To verify the simple bending theory.
  • To find the modulus of elasticity.
  • To determine the yield point in the case of mild steel and ‘modulus of rupture’ in the case of wood

Apparatus

  • Universal testing machine ( 40 / 5t capacity )
  • Dial indicators
  • Scale, sliding calipers.
